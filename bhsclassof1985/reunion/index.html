BHS Class of 1985 40 Year Reunion w/ Woodinville Sept 12 – 14, 2025 • 6-10pm • Nardoland, Bothell, WA

Welcome home, Cougars! Let’s make this weekend unforgettable.



Two High Schools. One Epic Class.
The Reunion We've Waited 40 Years For.


It was the story that defined our high school years: The Great Class Split. In Junior year, Woodinville High School opened, and our class was divided between Bothell and Woodinville.
We made new friends and new memories, but we never forgot the ones we started with.
Four decades later, the story gets a new chapter. The Bothell and Woodinville reunion committees have joined forces! MERGE WEEKEND from Friday, September 12th to Sunday, September 14th!
A historic evening where we bring both halves of our class back together again. Expect to see familiar faces you haven't seen since Junior year! This is more than a reunion; it's a reunification. Be there when the Class of '85 becomes whole again.

Weekend Overview

Join us for a weekend of celebration, memories, and fun with the two classes of '85.
The reunion committee is preparing a full itinerary, including:


  • Friday night music and dancing at the Lime
  • Saturday night dinner celebration at Nardoland (food by Class Act Catering)
  • Sunday picnic and games at Cottage Lake Park

Where?: Sat: Nardoland 22128 Paradise Lake Road, Snohomish

When?: Sat: 6-10pm
